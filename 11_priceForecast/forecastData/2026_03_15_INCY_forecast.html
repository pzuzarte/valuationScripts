CI bands useGARCH cumulative variance path

GARCH(1,1) models time-varying volatility: today's variance depends on yesterday's squared return (α) and yesterday's variance (β). High persistence means volatility shocks decay slowly. CI bands on the forecast chart use the h-step GARCH variance path rather than the flat σ×√h assumption, giving narrower bands in calm regimes and wider bands after large moves.
